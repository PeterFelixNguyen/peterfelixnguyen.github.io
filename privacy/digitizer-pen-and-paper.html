Digitizer Pen and Paper

Privacy Policy

WHAT Data This App Uses

This app uses Android Shared Preferences to store user settings.

Your Digitizer Pen and Paper Notes are stored as files in Android storage.

HOW This App Stores Your Data

Android Shared Preferences are stored locally on your Android file system. If you uninstall the app, all your selected settings will be cleared.

HOW This App Uses Your Data

All Shared Preferences data are accessed only by the app customize the app functionality.

WHO This App Shares Your Data With

This app does not share your Shared Preferences data.

No one is given access to your data

3rd Party Apps and Plugins

This app uses the AdMob network to display ads. AdMob is an ad network by Google.

https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en

Refer to Google's privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

App Permissions

This app requires permission to read, modify, and delete files created by the Digitizer Pen and Paper app.
